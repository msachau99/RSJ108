How to Become an Amazing Travel Journalist

Assembly time: 4 years
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Are you wondering on how to become a travel writer? These steps will help you get on the right footing and what you should do first! It doesn't happen over night but these directions will sure help you get on your way!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Laptop
  • Camera
  • Notebook or Journal
  • Phone
  • A Platform to Write On (Website, Blog, Word Processor, etc.)
  • Outgoing Personality & Ready to Travel!

  1. Read and do research!

    It is important to read and research work written by other travel writers. This can allow you to find your writing niche about what topic(s) you specifically want to focus on within travel writing.

  2. Start a blog or find your platform!

    It is important to start writing about where you have been, where you want to go, and what you recommend to others. Once you have pieces ready to publish, share them! Whether it's creating your own website or blog or even sharing them on Twitter, it is important to get your name out there.

    Important for future travel writers to always be writing!

  3. Look for stories!

    Think about what you have done in the past and what you can do in your own backyard. What do you see? What do you observe? Watch the video below to see how simple it is to start travel writing in the world around you.

  4. Take pictures!

    As important as it is to write, photos communicate more than words can say. Capture whatever you find interesting and eye-catching to strengthen your storytelling.

  5. Believe and invest in yourself!

    This profession is rewarding yet difficult at times, but rememeber to be open to new experiences and places. Keep an open mind and heart to the world.
